# Pocket Rocket

Pocket Rocket is a fast-paced 2D retro arcade shooter where you survive as long as possible in a hazardous asteroid field. Dodge incoming meteors, blast them to pieces, and collect power-ups to stay alive.

## Key Features

- **Retro Arcade Gameplay:** Fly your ship, shoot lasers, and survive the ever-increasing onslaught of meteors.
- **Neon Point Orbs:** Shoot special glowing orbs to pop them for massive bonus points before they float away.
- **Upgrade Shop:** Collect Flyons at the end of each run and spend them in the shop to upgrade your starting shields, maximum health, ship speed, bullet patterns, and item drop rate.
- **High Scores Leaderboard:** Compete with players worldwide and see your rank in real-time.
